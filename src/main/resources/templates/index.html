About Us

S.B. Jain Institute of Technology and Management Research, located in Nagpur, Maharashtra, aims to provide quality education in engineering and management disciplines. The institute focuses on holistic development, innovation, and research to prepare students for global challenges.

Vision

To be a leading institute in technical and management education, fostering innovation and entrepreneurship.

Mission

Empowering students with knowledge and skills through quality education, research, and industry collaboration.

Master of Technology (M.Tech)

Specializations: Computer Science, Mechanical, Electrical, Civil, Electronics & Communication.
